# # 📊 Customer Retention Strategy Dashboard

## 📝 Project Description

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior and identifying patterns that contribute to customer churn. Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard was developed to provide insights into customer retention, service usage, and account characteristics.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand key factors influencing churn and supports data-driven decision-making for improving customer retention strategies.

Live Dashboard - https://app.powerbi.com/links/OVoGbLK0qA?ctid=59f93a44-5dd6-43d0-8417-9dcd3c0e2773&pbi_source=linkShare

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn is a critical issue for businesses as it directly impacts revenue and growth. Identifying why customers leave and which segments are at higher risk is essential for designing effective retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall customer churn trends
* Identify key drivers of customer attrition
* Understand customer behavior across services and contracts
* Provide actionable insights to improve retention
* Enable interactive exploration of customer data

---

## 🗂️ Data Overview

The dataset includes customer-level information such as:

* Demographics (gender, dependents)
* Account details (contract type, tenure, payment method)
* Service usage (internet service, streaming, tech support)
* Financial metrics (monthly and yearly charges)
* Churn status

## 📈 Performance Score Logic

A basic analytical approach was used to evaluate customer segments based on:

* Contract type
* Monthly charges
* Tenure
* Service usage

Customers with short-term contracts and higher charges are generally considered more sensitive segments.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Month-to-month contracts represent a significant portion of customers
* Electronic check is the most commonly used payment method
* Customers in early tenure stages form a large segment
* Fiber optic service shows higher adoption among users
* Service usage varies significantly across customer groups

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Encourage long-term contracts through incentives
* Improve retention strategies for early-stage customers
* Optimize pricing strategies for premium services
* Target specific customer segments with personalized offers
* Enhance customer engagement through support services
